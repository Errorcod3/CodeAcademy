About Us

Originally founded in Germany in 1871, we moved our little cobble shop — Wenn der Schuh passt — stateside in 1900. In 1939 with the turmoil of WWII, we changed our name to If the Shoe Fits. We have been serving the feet of our neighbors for more than 200 years and we’ve enjoyed Fremont, Nebraska for more than 50 years. Whether you want a simple shine or a total resizing, If the Shoe Fits is here to serve you. From our family to yours, let’s make your feet happy.

- Master Cobbler David Hasselhoff
